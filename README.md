# 📊 KPI Performance Dashboard (Tableau)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **KPI Performance Dashboard** built using **Tableau** to monitor business performance across multiple dimensions.

The dashboard provides a high-level summary of key performance indicators along with geographic, categorical, and customer-level breakdowns to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this dashboard is to:

- Track overall business performance using key KPIs  
- Identify top-performing states and categories  
- Analyze customer contribution to revenue  
- Monitor profitability and discount trends  
- Enable filtering by category, region, and year  

This dashboard is designed for business managers, analysts, and executives to quickly evaluate performance and take strategic action.

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The top section of the dashboard highlights the following metrics:

- **Total Sales:** 2.30M  
- **Total Profit:** 0.29M  
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 37.87K  
- **Average Discount:** 15.62%  

Each KPI includes a trend visualization to monitor performance over time.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 1️⃣ Sales Across States (Geographical Analysis)

- Interactive US map visualization  
- State-level sales distribution  
- Darker shades indicate higher sales contribution  
- Helps identify high-performing and underperforming regions  

---

### 2️⃣ Sales Across Categories

- Compares sales performance across:
  - Furniture  
  - Office Supplies  
  - Technology  

- Enables category-level strategic analysis  
- Helps identify revenue-driving segments  

---

### 3️⃣ Top Customers

- Displays highest revenue-generating customers  
- Helps in:
  - Customer retention strategy  
  - Loyalty programs  
  - Targeted marketing campaigns  

---

### 4️⃣ Interactive Filters

The dashboard includes filters for:

- Category  
- Region  
- Year of Order Date  

These filters allow dynamic exploration of business performance.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Technology category contributes the highest sales.
- Certain states significantly outperform others in revenue generation.
- A small group of top customers drives a large share of total sales.
- Discount levels impact profitability trends.
